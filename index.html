Introduction:

Cyber Security and young people. (SFW)

An introduction

Cyber Security is important for everyone - however teaching such an in-depth and ever changing subject to children and young adults can be challenging. The aim of this website is to supplement this process by giving up-to-date information on a wide variety of issues relating to Cyber Security and the prevention of security breaches and malicious attacks.
These demographics are amongst the heaviest users of the internet; but also generally have a lower level of understanding of security risks and common malicious practices. This means that younger people on the internet are massively at risk, and it is important to educate them on how to avoid and prevent instances of security breaches.
